1. AI Ticket Filtering & Clustering

Quality

Compliance

Customer Satisfaction

2. AI Automatic Report Writing

Generates instant summaries for each complaint or cluster.

Produces professional supervisor-level reports including:

Root cause

Severity level

Affected users

Impact assessment

Recommended actions

3. AI Voice Interaction

Supports voice-based complaint input.

Detects emotional tone: anger, stress, urgency.

Responds empathetically and maintains conversation context.

Future-ready for enhanced voice emotion analysis.

4. AI Chatbot Enhancement

Intelligently understands complaint details.

Uses minimal questioning to extract essential information.

Maintains full conversation context across interactions.

Generates natural, contextual responses.

5. AI Routing

Automatically identifies the correct department or authority for each complaint.

Eliminates the need for users to know where to send their issue.

Handles exceptions, low-confidence routing, and escalation paths.

6. AI Dashboard Insights

Provides data for dashboards and analytics, including:

Complaint timelines

Delays and bottlenecks

Trending complaint clusters

Severity, sentiment, and priority metrics

7. AI Ticket Writing

Automatically generates complete ticket descriptions.

Predicts resolution time.

Assigns severity and priority levels.

8. AI Severity & Emotion Detection

Detects urgency and emotional intensity from text and voice.

Categorizes severity levels.

Automatically flags high-risk or high-emotion complaints for escalation.
